Common questions
Got questions?
How long does approval take? +
We review every application within 48 hours. You will receive a WhatsApp message or email from our team.
What products can I sell? +
Any original clothing, jewellery, accessories, or wearable art you have created. No reselling of other brands.
How do payouts work? +
Earnings released to your wallet 7 days after delivery. Withdraw any amount above ₹500 to your bank account anytime. Transfers take 1–3 business days.
Do I need a GST number? +
Not required to start. GST registration becomes mandatory if your annual revenue from all sources exceeds ₹20 lakh.
What is TCS? +
TCS at 1% is a mandatory deduction all Indian e-commerce operators must collect. It is passed to the Income Tax department on your behalf. You can claim it back when filing your ITR.
